THIS IS THE FIRST WEBSITE I HAVE EVER MADE

After being honored the privilege of taking part in overseeing and handling a 5 million dollar account I felt like I was able to gain the proper perspective on one way business can be conducted. It was very important for me to see how to take plans from the drawing board and seeing it out to completion. Although Belle's Gourmet Popcorn helped me become the man i am today I knew i need to be a part of something I truly believe in, so I decided to quit my job and transition into Full Stack Web Development.

Words can't accurately express enough how happy I am to have made this transition into software engineering. Even though I am aware I have made vast improvements in my coding knowledge and capabilities I know I have a lot to learn in regards to making efficient long lasting code. It can be intimidating applying for jobs in a brand new field, but I know improving my skills through gaining real life experience is the best way for me to learn. I am very excited to be part of a team again, and I cant wait to see what opportunities are out there for me.
